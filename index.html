My Vision

🌟

Meaningful Service

Create service opportunities that connect to your interests and make a real impact. No more checkbox volunteering—instead, we'll build projects that matter to both you and our community.

🚀

NHS Tutor Connect

I'm launching a platform that matches NHS tutors with students who need help. This creates meaningful service hours for members while supporting academic excellence at Franklin. The beta version is already connecting students across grade levels.

🎉

Community Integration

Transform NHS from an isolated club into a central hub that collaborates with other Franklin organizations. We'll coordinate with student groups, local businesses, and nonprofits to amplify our impact through strategic partnerships.

📱

Digital Efficiency

Streamline operations with a centralized system for tracking service hours, finding opportunities, and connecting with fellow members. Less time on paperwork means more time for what matters: actual service.

About Me

As your potential NHS President, I believe in substance over rhetoric. My goal is simple: transform our chapter into an organization that delivers genuine impact while developing each member's unique strengths.

Leadership isn't about padding resumes—it's about elevating others. I'm committed to building systems that amplify your voice and create opportunities that matter, both to you and to those we serve.
